7-Section Structure

Cover → Problem/Pain → Solution → How It Works → Why RPI → CTA → Contact. Consistent across all 5 product variants.

RPI Blue + Warm Imagery

RPI Blue (#4a7ab5), "We're Your People™", warm human photography. No cold stock photos. People who look like our clients.
