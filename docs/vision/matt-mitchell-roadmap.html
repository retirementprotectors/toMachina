Key Discussions

Comp Model

How the partnership compensation works. Street-level commissions, overrides, shared services fees, and how cross-sell revenue (retirement, life, legacy) flows back to you and your agents. Transparent, documented, no surprises.

Carrier Contract Migration

Which contracts stay under your hierarchy, which migrate to RPI’s, and which new contracts open up (Gradient, Schwab, BD relationships). The goal: your agents keep selling the carriers they know while gaining access to carriers they couldn’t access alone.

Data Integration Plan

How your client data comes into the platform. Our CIO (MEGAZORD) runs the ATLAS data pipeline — it’s built for exactly this: ingesting books of business from partner agencies. Client records, policy data, carrier feeds — mapped, normalized, and loaded into ProDashX.

Platform Access Rollout

Who gets access when. Phased rollout: you first (this weekend), then your staff, then your agents. Each tier gets training, documentation, and a dedicated onboarding contact. Nobody gets thrown into the deep end.

The 12-Week Merger Timeline

After Wednesday, we execute. 12 weeks from handshake to go-live. Each phase has clear deliverables, clear owners, and clear gates.

Weeks 1–2

Discovery Doc + Gap Analysis

MUSASHI (CMO) writes the formal Discovery Doc from Wednesday’s meeting — the same 8-tab format we use for every major initiative. Documents every domain, every gap, every decision point. Both sides sign off before a single line of code gets written or a single record gets migrated.

Weeks 3–4

Business Decisions

Weeks 5–6

Weeks 7–8

Weeks 9–10

Two weeks of running both systems side by side. Your team uses ProDashX for new business while keeping their existing tools for in-flight cases. Issues get caught and fixed in real time. RAIDEN (immune system) monitors for errors and auto-triages.

Weeks 11–12

Cutover + Go-Live

Old systems decommissioned. ProDashX is the single source of truth. C3 campaigns activated for Matt’s book. Proactive Service Model™ running. Welcome communications sent to clients. The Machine is live for Midwest Medigap.
