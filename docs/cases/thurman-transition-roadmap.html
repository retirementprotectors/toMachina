The Grandson Legacy Strategy — Family's Stated Intent

⭐ THE FAMILY'S WISH (PER FACT FINDER VERBATIM)

"They're hopeful to find a way to move some of their cash into a product that will grow the DB and the growth of the DB would be left to the grandson and the original principal would get split by the kids still."

Family Context

  • 3 kids — base inheritance recipients (split principal)
  • 1 grandson — works the big garage out back, intended to take over the farm
  • The fairness problem: Robert + Eileen wanted grandson to receive something meaningful (he's the farm successor) but not at the expense of equally splitting the rest among the 3 kids
  • The structural answer: separate the "grown DB" from the "original principal." Grandson gets the growth, kids get principal.

Structural Options (JDM Decides Product)

This structuring decision requires JDM consultation. Three structural avenues exist:

  1. Multi-beneficiary IUL with fixed-amount designation — IUL on Eileen's life is OUT (uninsurable). IUL on grandson's life (with Eileen funding) — possible but unusual. Better: this option may not fit given Eileen is uninsurable and Robert is gone.
  2. Wealth-transfer annuity with enhanced DB rider — annuity on Eileen with stepped-up DB feature. Grandson named beneficiary on full DB; principal returned to estate (and 3 kids) in a separate account. This works for an uninsurable owner.
  3. Two-product split structure — separate vehicles entirely: one to-grandson product (DB-focused), one to-kids product, premiums split at issue.

Constraints to discuss with JDM:
• Eileen is the only living owner-candidate
• Eileen is uninsurable (rules out IUL on her life)
• Available cash is ~$130-140K (depending on final claim payouts)
• Grandson is presumed not an Eligible Designated Beneficiary under SECURE Act 2.0 (10-yr drain rule applies for inherited IRAs; less restrictive for non-IRA products)

What this roadmap does NOT recommend: a specific product. Doctrine: surface the structural requirement and option space. Product selection at this complexity is JDM territory.
