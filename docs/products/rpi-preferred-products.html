Why we lead with this

  • Premium bonus of up to 21% (12% base + 9% EBR rider) on premiums received in first 3 years — instantly offsets surrender charges from 1035 exchanges of older contracts
  • 18 uncapped index options + 6 volatility-control indexes (Morgan Stanley, Fidelity, S&P, Goldman Sachs, Barclays)
  • 10% annual penalty-free withdrawal starting year 2 — handles RMDs cleanly
  • 100% accumulation value available if confined to qualified care facility (LTC-style waiver)
Pitch language: "Your CD is paying you to lose to inflation. We can move that money into a North American contract, pick up an immediate premium bonus, and let it grow tax-deferred with zero market risk — and if you ever need it for care, you get 100% of it back without surrender."

The stacking lever

  • Benefit base doubles on a schedule: 120% of premium in years 1–5, 150% in 6–10, 180% in years 11+ — with floors of $120K/$150K/$180K on a $100K premium
  • Income rider fires off the benefit base, not the accumulation value — pure income multiplier
  • Ideal for clients in their mid-60s wanting a guaranteed income stream in their late 70s
  • Rider can stack with LTC doubler (state-dependent — confirm Iowa availability)
Pitch language: "Let's cook this for 10 years. On day one, your income base is $120K. Year 11, your income base is $180K — that's an 80% lift on the money you pay income off of, guaranteed, regardless of what the market does. And if you need long-term care, that number doubles again."
