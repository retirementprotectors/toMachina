VOLTRON Icon Concepts

Four directions for VOLTRON's new icon — replacing the current smart_toy Material Icon in the ProDash sidebar. JDM: pick your favorite direction.

Context

VOLTRON is the team's AI assistant — the "BFF" who lives in the ProDash sidebar panel. Currently uses a generic smart_toy Material Icon. JDM said twice on the leadership call: "I got to get a better logo for him."

The name comes from Voltron: Defender of the Universe (1984) — five robot lions that combine into one mega robot. Key visual DNA: the V-shaped chest, bold primary colors (blue, red, green, yellow, black), the Blazing Sword, and the transformation sequence.

Design Constraints

ConstraintRequirement
Size24×24px sidebar icon (must read at small size)
FormatSVG (vector, scalable)
ColorWorks on dark background (#0f1117), uses VOLTRON blue (#3b82f6) as primary
StyleMust match the sidebar icon weight of other nav items
BrandMust feel like VOLTRON — not generic AI/robot
ProductionJDM art-directs. MUSASHI does NOT generate logos — we source artwork per brand standards.

Four Concept Directions

Direction A
🦁

The Lion Head

A stylized lion head — the most iconic element of the original Voltron. The Black Lion leads the formation. Simple silhouette, reads at 24px, instantly communicates "VOLTRON" to anyone who knows the reference.
Vibe: Bold, commanding, recognizable
Reference: Black Lion face from GoLion/Voltron
Best for: Instant brand recognition
Direction B
⚔️

The Blazing Sword

VOLTRON's signature weapon — the Blazing Sword appears in every episode's climax. A stylized V-shaped sword or blade icon. Clean, sharp, minimal. "Form Blazing Sword!" is the most iconic catchphrase.
Vibe: Action, power, decisive
Reference: The glowing blade from the transformation sequence
Best for: Clean sidebar icon, strong silhouette
Direction C
🛡️

The V-Shield

The "V" from VOLTRON rendered as a shield or badge shape. Combines the letter V with a protective/defender motif. Modern, geometric, works great at small sizes. "Defender of the Universe" energy.
Vibe: Protective, trustworthy, modern
Reference: V-shaped chest plate + shield concept
Best for: Professional feel, scales well, versatile
Direction D
🤖

The Mecha Head

VOLTRON's robot head — the combined mecha form with the distinctive helmet/crown shape, angular jawline, and glowing eyes. More detailed than the other options but unmistakably VOLTRON when you see it.
Vibe: Powerful, complete, heroic
Reference: Combined Voltron robot face/helmet
Best for: Maximum "that's VOLTRON" impact
MUSASHI's Recommendation: Direction C (V-Shield) for the sidebar icon. It reads cleanest at 24px, feels professional, and the "V" is unmistakable. Direction A (Lion Head) is the strongest brand statement if you want more personality. Both work — it's a vibe call.

Black Lion Deep Dive — 3 Approaches

JDM is leaning lion. Here's how a Black Lion icon works on our dark sidebar. Each preview shows the COLOR + TREATMENT approach — final artwork will be a proper lion illustration sourced by MUSASHI.

Approach 1
🦁

Blue-Outlined

Dark fill, VOLTRON blue (#3b82f6) outline. The blue edge pops on dark backgrounds. Same energy the OG show used — the Black Lion always had highlights around it.
Vibe: Clean, professional, high contrast
Best for: Sidebar icon at 24px
Approach 2
🦁

Glowing Eyes

Near-invisible dark lion with glowing blue eyes. Dramatic. The eyes become the icon at small sizes. Animated pulse adds life. "The Black Lion emerges from darkness."
Vibe: Dramatic, mysterious, alive
Best for: Maximum cool factor
Approach 3
🦁

Light Silhouette

White/light lion silhouette that represents the Black Lion through shape alone. At 24px, shape matters more than color. Clean, bold, works on any background.
Vibe: Bold, readable, versatile
Best for: Maximum readability at any size
MUSASHI's pick: Approach 1 (Blue-Outlined) for the sidebar. Approach 2 (Glowing Eyes) is the coolest but may be too subtle at 24px. Approach 3 (Light Silhouette) is the safest.

Note: The lion emoji above shows the COLOR TREATMENT — not the final art. Once JDM picks an approach, we source a proper stylized lion illustration and apply the chosen treatment.
