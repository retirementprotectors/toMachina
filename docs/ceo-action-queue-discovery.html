The Problem

JDM is the approval bottleneck for the entire platform. Bugs, features, case files, sprint gates — everything needs a CEO decision at some point. Right now those decisions happen via:

  • Slack messages buried in channel history
  • tmux sessions that can't be copy-pasted
  • Google Sheets nobody checks
  • Verbal conversations that aren't tracked
The Vision: Any decision that needs JDM's eyes lands in a single queue. He opens one URL on his phone — from an airplane, a client meeting, anywhere — and goes approve, decline, approve, reclassify, done. 60 seconds. The Machine moves forward.

The Solution: /q

A dedicated route at prodash.tomachina.com/q — a mobile-first decision queue. Cards stack vertically. Huge tap targets. No nav, no sidebar, no resizing. Just the queue.
