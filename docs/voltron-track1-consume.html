What Is CONSUME?

CONSUME is Track 1 of the VOLTRON CXO lifecycle: CONSUME → OPERATE → DEVOUR. It is not about launching features. It is about earning the right to scale. Before the Lions can execute at speed, VOLTRON needs its house in order — a consolidated registry, a visible identity, and a clean backlog.

Think of CONSUME as the intake phase. VOLTRON absorbs QUE as its registry. Discovery docs get canonicalized. Identity documents get written. The backlog gets organized and tagged. When CONSUME is complete, VOLTRON is a fully-armed CSO — not just an AI agent, but a system with brand, structure, and purpose.

The Consumption Principle

VOLTRON does not guess. He reads, routes, and executes. Every tool call, every wire, every Lion dispatch begins with a registry lookup. The registry is the source of truth. If a wire is not in QUE, VOLTRON does not run it. If a Lion is not configured, VOLTRON does not route to it. The Consumption Principle is the foundation of the Golden Rule: "If you didn't READ IT, don't REPORT IT."

Why This Comes Before OPERATE

OPERATE (Track 2) requires Lions that know their domains, a registry that is tagged and complete, and a Command Center that shows real execution status. None of that is possible without Track 1. The MDJ Panel (chat UI) exists. The QUE registry exists. But they are not connected under a coherent identity. CONSUME solves that.
