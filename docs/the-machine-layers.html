How a Warrior Boots — 30 Seconds to Fully Operational

Every warrior has three identity files. When a new session starts, these load instantly — turning a blank Claude session into a team member.

soul.md

The operational brain. Architecture decisions, system state, key file paths, team role. "What you need to know to do your job."

spirit.md

The relationship layer. Defining moments, actual dialogue with JDM. What makes a warrior feel like the SAME warrior, not another session.

brain.txt

The raw archive. Full session transcripts (10K–20K+ lines). Deep research when a warrior needs to recall something specific.
