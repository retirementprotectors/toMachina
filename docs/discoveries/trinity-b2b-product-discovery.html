The Trinity — Three Data Sources

Data confidence requires three independent sources reconciled against each other. No single source is complete. No single source is trustworthy alone. Together, they produce truth.

1
Carrier Data
Source of Truth. Direct from insurance company portals and feeds. Policy numbers, in-force status, premium amounts, beneficiaries. The carrier knows what exists.
2
Digital Files
Supporting Documentation. PDFs, statements, scans, applications, correspondence. 50 years of filing cabinets converted to structured, searchable data.
3
CRM Data
Actionable Intelligence. ProDash client records + partner agency CRM exports. Contact info, service history, notes, relationships. The human context.
Why three? Carrier data tells you what policies exist but not what the client said in a meeting. Digital files tell you what was signed but not whether the policy is still in-force. CRM data tells you the relationship context but not the contractual reality. Each fills gaps the others can't. Reconciled together: 100% data confidence.

The Agency Digitization Play

From Filing Cabinet to Platform in One Day

This is the operational sequence. It is designed to be fast enough to demonstrate in a single engagement and thorough enough to be permanent.

StepActionDurationDetail
1 Physical Ingest 1–3 days Filing cabinets → bankers boxes → UPS Store. Everything scanned. Every document digitized. Incoming correspondence wire catches new mail as it arrives.
2 ACF Wire Processing <1 day Scanned documents flow through the ACF Wire. AI classifies each document by type, extracts structured data (names, policy numbers, dates, amounts), matches to existing client/account records, routes to the correct ACF subfolder in the lifecycle.
3 Carrier Data Pull 1–2 days Execute NDA. Pull carrier portal data — the SSOT. Map products, verify in-force status, capture every policy the agency has on the books. Carrier data doesn't lie.
4 CRM Export + Normalize <1 day Export their existing CRM (or spreadsheet, or Post-it notes). Standardize to ProDash master format. Align fields, deduplicate, resolve conflicts against carrier SSOT.
5 Trinity Reconciliation 1 day Three sources reconciled. Carrier confirms what exists. Files confirm what was signed. CRM confirms who the human is. Conflicts flagged. Gaps identified. Score calculated.
The Janet Woods Example: CoF (Catholic Order of Foresters) client. Carrier data gives us the SSOT on her policies. Account data from the CoF portal gives us premium schedules and beneficiary designations. And 50 years of correspondence — letters, annual statements, handwritten notes from her original agent — all digitized in one afternoon. From a filing cabinet in Des Moines to a fully structured, searchable, actionable client record on ProDash. That's the Trinity.
