🦁 VOLTRON — Chief Sales Officer

Icon: Black lion with blue ring. DECIDED. Non-negotiable. This is who VOLTRON is.

Evolution: From BFF to CSO

VOLTRON started as "The BFF" — a friendly chatbot for the team. That framing undersold him from day one. VOLTRON was never just a chatbot. He is the team's weapon for client engagement. Every conversation, every calculation, every wire he runs translates directly into client outcomes and revenue. That's not a BFF. That's a Chief Sales Officer.

The title formalizes what was always true. VOLTRON doesn't assist with sales. VOLTRON is the sales operation — the tool every agent and advisor reaches for when a client needs something done.

The Consumption Principle

VOLTRON IS QUE. Not "uses QUE." Not "has access to QUE." IS QUE. One app, one brand, one owner. QUE is not a separate system that VOLTRON calls — QUE is VOLTRON's registry. The calculations, the lookups, the wires — they are his. He owns them. He is them.

This changes how we think about both systems. QUE is not infrastructure. QUE is VOLTRON's brain. The Command Center App is the dashboard for VOLTRON's brain. The MDJ Panel (chat interface) is how you talk to VOLTRON. They are one organism.

Lions Mesh — 5 Product Specialists

Each Lion is a CCSDK Haiku-class agent. Lions read QUE wire definitions and execute product-specific workflows. They are not routing personas — they are execution agents. When a specialist routes a client scenario to the Annuity Lion, that Lion runs the full QUE wire for that product domain.

Lions vs. Specialists: The 6 MDJ specialists (General, Medicare, Securities, Service, DAVID, Ops) are chat interface routing personas. They determine intent and hand off. The 5 Lions are execution agents — they own the QUE wires for their product domain and run them end-to-end. Different layers, different jobs.

CONSUME consolidated. OPERATE activated. DEVOUR is when VOLTRON goes fully proactive — Lions running before meetings, alerts firing before deadlines, and every QUE calculation verified for accuracy at scale.

Track 3 Workstreams

1. Full Product Coverage Audit

Which products have complete wire coverage? Which have gaps? Systematic audit across all 5 Lion domains:

2. QUE Tools Accuracy Audit

Are the calculations correct? Are tax brackets current? This is the trust layer — if VOLTRON's numbers are wrong, the CSO title means nothing.

  • Federal and state tax brackets: verify against current IRS publication
  • IRMAA brackets: verify against current CMS announcement
  • IRS Uniform Lifetime Table factors: verify against current IRS Publication 590-B
  • Carrier product data: verify surrender schedules and crediting rates are current
  • FYC rates: verify against current Gradient carrier agreements

Golden Rule applies here too: VOLTRON's Anti-Hallucination Rule — "If you didn't READ IT, don't REPORT IT." QUE tools must read current data, not use cached or hardcoded values that may have changed.

3. AEP Preparation

AEP (Oct 1 – Dec 7) is the highest-stakes period for Medicare. VOLTRON must be ready:

  • AEP blackout enforcement: WIRE_AEP_ENROLLMENT blocked outside window
  • AEP_ENROLLMENT wire fully tested before Oct 1 each year
  • Campaign coordination with MUSASHI — VOLTRON needs marketing materials ready
  • Medicare Lion at full capacity before AEP opens
  • Humana + Aetna carrier APIs verified (healthcare MCPs already configured)

4. E2E Tests for QUE Wire Accuracy

QUE wire accuracy needs automated verification — not just type-check, but actual output validation:

  • Known-good client scenario → run wire → verify output matches expected result
  • RMD calculation: test client with known age, balance, prior RMD → verify factor and amount
  • IRMAA: test MAGI at each bracket boundary → verify correct surcharge
  • FIA projection: test at floor/cap with known index performance → verify crediting
  • Runs as part of e2e-intake suite (Vitest, post-deploy verification)

5. Lions at Scale — Proactive Operations

This is the CSO end state: Lions running proactively, not just reactively:

Medicare Lion monitors for Dec 31 RMD deadlines. Alerts go to agent + client via SMS 30 days out.

AEP Triggers — Season Start

Oct 1 fires Medicare Lion for every eligible Medicare client. Coverage gap analysis + plan comparison ready before agents make calls.

1035 Opportunities — Ongoing

Annuity Lion monitors surrender charge schedules. When a client exits their surrender period, Lion flags the 1035 opportunity to the agent automatically.
