Section 03

Funnel Architecture

Every CTA on every page follows the same one-way funnel into Sentinel. The audience tag is set at the URL level — never by user input. Zero friction, zero ambiguity.

Step 1 — Entry
Sniper-Fire URL
Partner receives a direct deep link. JS opens exact sub-tab and example on load.
↓
Step 2 — Engagement
CTA on page
One CTA per sub-tab. No nav, no distractions. CTA triggers the lead form modal.
↓
Step 3 — Capture
Lead form modal
Minimal fields. Audience tag is pre-set from the URL path — visitor never sees or touches it.
↓
Step 4 — Submit
POST services/api/leads/<audience>
Form posts to the toMachina API lead intake route. Audience tag is in the endpoint path — tamper-proof.
↓
Step 5 — Storage
Sentinel · intake_queue
Lead writes to Sentinel's intake_queue Firestore collection with: audience tag, source URL, timestamp, contact fields.
↓
Step 6 — Routing
DAVIDHUB — Routed by audience tag
Matt McCormick and team work leads from DAVIDHUB. Tag determines queue, template, and team member assignment.
The 7 audience tags
agency (B2B) firm (B2E) carrier (B2I) strategic platform rpi general
