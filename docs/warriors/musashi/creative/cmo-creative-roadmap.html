FYF Strategy

The CMO Mission

Equip every RPI division with client-facing materials that make each team member 10x more effective.

Build content once per market. Deliver it across every channel. Scale through acquisition. Every agency Matt brings in via DAVID gets the same materials on Day 1.

The Architecture: Markets x Divisions x Channels

Markets are the WHAT — what the content says. A Medicare brochure is a Medicare brochure whether it's printed, emailed, or displayed in ProDashX.

Divisions are the WHO — who needs it. Sales team (Vince) vs. Service team (Nikki) vs. DAVID (Matt).

Channels are the HOW — how it gets delivered. Digital, Web, Print, Social, Email.

The rule: Content is market-specific first. Channels are the output format. Build the message once, deliver everywhere.
