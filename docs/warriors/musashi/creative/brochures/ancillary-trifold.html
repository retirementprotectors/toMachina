Ancillary · The Gaps

Three Gaps People Miss

Every Medicare plan has the same three blind spots. Here's what's missing — and what fills each gap.

Gap 1

The daily gap

Dental, vision, hearing — Medicare doesn't cover routine care. Dental/Vision/Hearing plans exist as standalone policies or as extras inside Medicare Advantage. Most have no waiting period for preventive care.

Gap 2

The catastrophic gap

Long-term care costs $90K+/year nationally. Medicare covers skilled nursing only — and only the first 100 days. After day 100, the bill is 100% yours. LTC insurance and Hospital Indemnity both address this exposure.

Gap 3

The final gap

Medicare stops at death. Burial, cremation, and remaining medical bills fall to your family. Final Expense Life policies ($5K–$25K) are designed specifically for this — simplified underwriting, guaranteed benefit, premiums fixed for life.
