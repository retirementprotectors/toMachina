8. Logo Usage

D&Z rule: The logo variations D&Z provided are the only acceptable variations and should not be altered. Use Black/White only as required. Over dark backgrounds, the logo can be used white.
NEVER (from D&Z):
  • Rotate or angle the logo — always on a horizontal baseline of 90°.
  • Fade or use transparency settings.
  • Skew or stretch any elements.
  • Apply a stroke or outline.
  • Use unapproved colors.
  • Recreate the logo with another typeface.
  • Add drop shadow or other effects.
NEVER generate, approximate, or "whip up" an RPI logo. JDM art-directs every logo. Use authorized marks from packages/ui/src/logos/ or the Canva Brand Kit only.

Safety Area & Minimum Size

RuleSpec
Safety area (clear space)Space equal to a square of half the logo's height (x/2) on all sides.
Minimum size — print22 mm (0.84 in)
Minimum size — screen61 px (at 72 dpi)
ScalingWhen possible, scale/proportion determined by available space and alignment to layout grid.
