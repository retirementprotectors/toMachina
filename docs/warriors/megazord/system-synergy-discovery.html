What Is System Synergy?

The observability + hygiene layer for The Machine itself. It answers three questions:

  1. Is the knowledge pipeline flowing? — Are warriors getting smarter across sessions?
  2. Is the platform healthy? — Deploys, hooks, sessions, duplicates, orphans?
  3. Where are the gaps? — What questions can't VOLTRON answer? What's stale?

The Problem

The Learning Loop and Warrior Wisdom systems are half-built. The pipes exist but aren't connected:

Key insight: Claude Code has 8 built-in persistence layers. Layers 1-4 are automatic raw data. Layers 5-8 are curated instruction. Nothing moves data from 1-4 into 5-8 automatically. That's the gap.

How the Learning Loop SHOULD Work

Platform-wide knowledge that ALL warriors need. Business rules, compliance, architecture decisions, client patterns, process changes.

SESSION | v auto-memory writes to MEMORY.md (Claude built-in) | v entity-extractor (daily 2am) reads warrior soul/spirit/brain files | v Firestore knowledge_entries (confidence-scored) | v knowledge-promote (daily 4am) queries high-confidence entries | v AUTO-WRITES to CLAUDE.md (not just Slack DM) | v gap-analyzer (daily 3am) finds blind spots | v Next session is smarter

How Warrior Wisdom SHOULD Work

Per-warrior context that makes THAT warrior smarter across sessions. Their decisions, their style, their domain knowledge, their relationship with JDM.

SESSION | +--> soul.md (operational brain, ~400 lines, loaded every session) +--> spirit.md (relationship + personality, ~400 lines, loaded every session) +--> brain.txt (raw archive, append-only, feeds entity extractor) +--> MEMORY.md (auto-memory, loaded by Claude Code automatically)
