Income Is Secured. This Is About Legacy.

Your $12,900/month income is locked in — that doesn't change regardless of which option you choose. The only decision is how much additional portfolio income to redirect toward creating a larger tax-free inheritance for your family.

Option A requires virtually nothing beyond the Nassau remainder and PRU Term savings. Options B and C tap a portion of portfolio gains — money that would otherwise be taxable to your heirs — and convert it into tax-free death benefit at roughly 50:1 leverage.
