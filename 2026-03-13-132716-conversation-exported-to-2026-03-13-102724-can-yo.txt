  which GHL stage it came from. It's purely metadata on the Firestore doc — not
  in any Sheet.

  The "Separate Mapping Doc" option would mean instead of putting it on every
  stage, we'd just have one reference document that maps old GHL IDs to new flow
   stage IDs. Either way it's Firestore, just a question of where the data
  lives.
