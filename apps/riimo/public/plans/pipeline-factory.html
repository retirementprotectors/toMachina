Gate Enforcement Model

Hard Gate (gate_enforced: true) — Cannot advance. All required tasks must pass.
No Gate (gate_enforced: false) — Free movement (drag-drop Kanban).
Soft Gate (future) — Warning but allows override with reason logged.
