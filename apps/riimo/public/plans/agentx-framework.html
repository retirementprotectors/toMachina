Why These Words

Every word was chosen by elimination. The wrong words told us what the right words needed to be. Nothing survived that didn't earn its place.

The Platform

Not "powerful." Not "innovative." Not "cutting-edge."
Transcendent

Beyond comparison. A transcendent platform doesn't live in one industry, doesn't live in one channel, and has its own immune system. It's not a tech platform that's really good — it's a tech platform that's alive.

What The Machine Does

Not "empowers." Not "enables." Not "supports."
Arms

"Empowers" is what every corporate deck on earth says. "Arms" is what we actually mean — we're giving our people weapons nobody else has.

The Service

Not "excellent." Not "world-class." Not "best-in-class."
Unrivaled

There's no sentence that starts with "but we—" because there's nobody on the other side of that comparison. The conversation is over before it starts.

What DAVID Does

Not "creates." Not "builds." Not "conquers."
Forges

One person. Raw materials. Heat. Pressure. Will. "Forges" is creation through fire — and it's inherently a one-person word. One blacksmith, one hammer, one vision.

What The Magic Does

Not "compounds." Not "catalyzes." Not "unleashes."
Ignites

The money is fuel. The machine is the engine. The magic is the ignition. Without it, everything just sits there. With it, the flywheel starts burning — and it never stops.
