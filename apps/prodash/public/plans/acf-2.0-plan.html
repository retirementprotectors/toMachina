Architecture

4 Intake Sources → intake_queue (Firestore) → Wire Trigger → WIRE_INCOMING_CORRESPONDENCE
  ├── MAIL (5 min scan)         Reactive hook (immediate on upload)
  ├── SPC_INTAKE (5 min scan)   Proactive scan (hourly, incremental)
  ├── ACF_UPLOAD (immediate)    Belt + suspenders coverage
  └── ACF_SCAN (hourly)
