Risk AreaLevelMitigation
Client merge Medium Dry run first — JDM reviews all proposed merges before live execution. Every merge logged to audit trail. Reversible.
Account merge Medium Dry run first — preview all merges with full before/after comparison. Policy number + carrier must be exact match.
