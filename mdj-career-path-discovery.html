The Concept

MDJ-Coach is a training-focused specialist that knows every team member's position on the career path, tracks their activity and production metrics in real time, and proactively guides them toward the next level. It replaces the LMS, the onboarding coordinator, and the manager's weekly 1:1 check-in — simultaneously, for every employee, at every agency.

The Big Idea

This could be the #1 retention and development tool at scale. If MDJ-Coach makes every employee feel like they have a dedicated career coach on call 24/7, churn drops and performance climbs.

1

Career Path Intelligence

Knows the exact requirements for each level — production metrics, certifications, competencies, time-in-role. Can answer "what do I need to do to reach Specialist?" with a specific, personalized action plan based on the user's actual current state.

5

Advancement Tracking

"To reach Specialist, you need X, Y, and Z. You've completed X and Y. Here's what closing Z looks like, and here's the timeline if you maintain current pace." Makes advancement feel concrete, not ambiguous.

The Problem MDJ Solves

Traditional agency integration after an acquisition takes 3–6 months. You need to manually migrate client data, retrain agents on new systems, re-paper accounts with carriers, and rebuild operational muscle memory. At 24 acquisitions in 2026 and 144 in 2027, that timeline makes 181-agency scale functionally impossible.

The Math

At 3 months per integration, 144 acquisitions in 2027 would require 432 person-months of integration work — simultaneously. MDJ-powered integration cuts this to 7 days.

The 7-Day Integration Playbook

Day 0

Deal Closes

DAVID closes the acquisition. SENTINEL pipeline moves to "Closed/Won." MDJ-DAVID automatically creates the integration project and triggers the onboarding sequence.

Day 1

BoB Intake Pipeline Created

MDJ-DAVID creates the ATLAS intake pipeline for the book of business. Carrier data export requested. RAPID_IMPORT wire definitions mapped to the acquired agency's data format.

Day 2

ATLAS Wires Ingest the BoB

Clients, accounts, carriers, and commissions flow through ATLAS normalization. Data is validated against RAPID_CORE schemas. MDJ-Integration monitors for errors and flags exceptions in real time.

Day 3

Data Normalized & Matched

DTCC feeds pulled. Client records matched to existing RPI data where possible (avoiding duplicates). Commission structures mapped to RPI comp table. All records written to Firestore.

Day 4

Every Client Visible in ProDashX

100% of acquired clients are live in ProDashX. Service team can immediately see full client history, accounts, and action items. Zero data is lost. RPI is now actively servicing the acquired BoB.

Day 5

Acquired Agents Get MDJ Access

Firebase accounts provisioned. Agents see their entire book of business immediately. MDJ-Coach welcomes them with a personalized onboarding plan based on their role and production history.

Day 7

Operational — Full RPI Service Delivery

MDJ-Coach has walked every acquired agent through RPI workflows. First client outreach queue is populated. Compliance requirements confirmed. Integration complete. Agents are productive on Day 7.
